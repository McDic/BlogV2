---
title: QUIZ 4. Four Single Digit Numbers
---

I've found two answers so far.

- $1, 3, 8, 9$
    - $10 = 1 + 9$
    - $20 = 9 \times 3 - 8 + 1$
    - $30 = 3 \times (1 + 9)$
    - $40 = 8 \times (9 - 3 - 1)$
    - $50 = 3 \times (9 + 8) - 1$
    - $60 = 9 \times (8 - 1) - 3$
    - $70 = 9 \times 8 - 3 + 1$
    - $80 = 8 \times (9 + 1)$
    - $90 = 9 \times (8 + 3 - 1)$
- $2, 2, 5, 8$ (Probably the most intuitive answer)
    - $10 = 2 \times 5$
    - $20 = 8 \times 5 \div 2$
    - $30 = (8 - 2) \times 5$
    - $40 = 8 \times 5$
    - $50 = 5 \times (8 + 2)$
    - $60 = 5 \times 2 \times (8 - 2)$
    - $70 = 5 \times (8 \times 2 - 2)$
    - $80 = 5 \times 8 \times 2$
    - $90 = 5 \times (8 \times 2 + 2)$

I haven't bruteforced this yet so there may be other answers.
